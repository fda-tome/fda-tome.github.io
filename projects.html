Projects

Lead Researcher | 2025–present

BOP (Balanced Orthogonal Propagation)

Devising a novel wave propagation method using red-blue pebbling scheduling techniques (COSMA-like) to accelerate seismic imaging and leverage accelerators (GPUs).

Researcher | 2022–2024

Acoustic Frozen Waves (AFW)

Ran high-performance simulations of acoustic frozen waves to validate partial-wave models, achieving 31x faster runtimes and demonstrating applications such as volumetric displays (holograms).

Maintainer | 2024-present

Dagger.jl

Maintained Dagger.jl as part of a two-person team, implemented the MPI backend, and scaled distributed runs to 500+ nodes on the Aurora supercomputer. Built multi-GPU array support, added data dependency annotations, and delivered QR/LU/Cholesky factorizations that rival vendor libraries.

Co-creator | 2025

NextLA.jl

Implemented base kernels and the CAQR factorization sub-kernels for a suite of high-performance linear algebra routines.

Contributor | 2024–present

SmartSolve.jl

Built a benchmark suite that compares against all PETSc solver types and all backends to evaluate performance tradeoffs.
